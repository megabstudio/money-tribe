One-line: Pill badge for status, counts, and category tags.

```jsx
<Badge>On track</Badge>
<Badge variant="solid">New</Badge>
<Badge variant="dark">Pro</Badge>
<Badge variant="destructive">Overdue</Badge>
```
Variants: `primary` (soft green), `solid`, `dark`, `neutral`, `outline`, `destructive`. Sizes `sm|md`.
